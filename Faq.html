Frequently Asked Questions


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all credit cards, Paypal, and Apple Pay.








What is your return policy?

We love money so we are very hesitant to allow returns, however if you contact us within 30 days you are elegible for a return as long as the item is in the original condition.








Do you offer international shipping?

We offer domestic, international, and even intergalactic shipping.








How do I know what size to order?

We provide a size chart on each product page to help you find your best fit.








Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed?

We'll think about it.








How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary based on your location and shipping method selected at checkout.








Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package.








What if I receive a damaged or defective item?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will provide a prepaid return label and issue a refund or exchange.








How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team by email at support@TGS.com or by phone at 1-800-555-5555. We're available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST.
