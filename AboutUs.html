About Us


"The Golden Snubs, or TGS for short, was founded in 2023 by a group of friends who shared a passion for fashion and a desire to create a brand that was truly unique. The founders, Hansaint Noto, Carlos Posadas, Abdurrehman Aslam, and Ghazi Harara, came together with a vision of creating a clothing line that would stand out from the crowd."




"Although we have only been in business for over 3 days, our mission at TGS is clear: to provide well-made, quality clothing for a price that will turn us into millionaires. We believe that the best part about purchasing our clothes is that no other company has anything even remotely as stylish. At TGS, we are committed to providing our customers with the most fashionable and unique clothing available. We are excited to be a part of the fashion industry and look forward to growing our brand and serving our customers for years to come."

-Ghazi Harara when questioned about the future goals for TGS






"At TGS, we understand that fashion is not just about looking good, it's also about feeling good. That's why we take great care in selecting the materials we use for our clothing. We believe that quality and comfort should never be sacrificed for style. Our commitment to using only the best materials ensures that our customers not only look good but also feel good in our clothing. We are dedicated to providing a shopping experience that is both enjoyable and satisfying, and we are always striving to exceed our customers' expectations. At TGS, we are more than just a fashion brand, we are a lifestyle."



"TGS, give us your money now."
